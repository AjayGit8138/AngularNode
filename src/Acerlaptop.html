Product Description

Acer Aspire 5 (A514-54) is a Windows 11 laptop with a 14.00-inch display. It is powered by a Core i5 processor and it comes with 8GB of RAM. The Acer Aspire 5 (A514-54) packs 512GB of SSD storage. Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/Yes, Bluetooth and it comes with 4 USB ports, USB 3.2 Gen 1 (Type A), HDMI Port, RJ45 (LAN) ports. As of 21st February 2022, Acer Aspire 5 (A514-54) price in India starts at Rs. 56,990.
